# CD4 Target Discovery Portal — 3-minute demo storyboard

## Brief

- Audience: biomedical researchers, translational scientists, clinicians evaluating research evidence, and project reviewers.
- Format: 1920 × 1080 landscape screen recording.
- Hard limit: 3 minutes.
- Core message: one genome-scale CD4 Perturb-seq screen becomes an auditable target-prioritisation workspace without hiding uncertainty or collapsing research evidence into clinical advice.
- Narrative arc: orient → discover → inspect → translate → verify → reproduce.

## Recording notes

- Record the browser video without live narration first.
- Record each narration block as a separate audio take; this makes timing corrections easy.
- Keep browser zoom at 100% and viewport at 1920 × 1080.
- Use cuts between screens rather than long scrolling transitions.
- Keep every click-to-settle pause below one second.
- End on Provenance rather than Home.
