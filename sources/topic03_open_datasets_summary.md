# Topic 3 open datasets summary

This file records the open dataset search performed on 2026-07-04 for Topic 3: comparable open data for GWT CD4 Perturb-seq drug-development use cases.

Key open-access anchors:

- Primary Human CD4+ T Cell Perturb-seq, CZI Virtual Cells: genome-scale CRISPRi Perturb-seq in primary human CD4 T cells, >22M cells, 4 donors, 3 stimulation conditions. URL: https://virtualcellmodels.cziscience.com/dataset/genome-scale-tcell-perturb-seq
- GSE119450: CROP-seq in primary human T cells, CD8 T cells from two donors, stimulated and unstimulated, 48 sgRNAs. URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE119450
- GSE92872: CROP-seq Jurkat TCR activation, 5,905 high-quality single-cell transcriptomes with uniquely assigned gRNAs. URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE92872
- Frangieh/Melms Perturb-CITE-seq, SCP1064: melanoma and autologous TIL co-culture, ~218,000 cells, ~750 tumor-intrinsic perturbations, RNA and 20 surface proteins. URL: https://singlecell.broadinstitute.org/single_cell/study/SCP1064/multi-modal-pooled-perturb-cite-seq-screens-in-patient-models-define-novel-mechanisms-of-cancer-immune-evasion
- Dixit/Parnas/Regev Perturb-seq, GSE90063/SCP31: 200,000 cells, BMDC LPS and K562 TF perturbations. URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE90063
- Adamson et al. Perturb-seq UPR, GSE90546/CZI Virtual Cells: K562 UPR CRISPRi Perturb-seq. URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE90546
- Norman et al. Perturb-seq CRISPRa combinations, GSE133344 and figshare h5ad. URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE133344
- Replogle et al. genome-scale Perturb-seq, PRJNA831566/gwps.wi.mit.edu/Figshare/CZI Virtual Cells: >2.5M cells in K562/RPE1. URL: http://gwps.wi.mit.edu
- sci-Plex3 chemical perturbation, GSE139944/CELLxGENE/scPerturb/pertpy: ~650,000 cells, A549/K562/MCF7, 188 compounds, 4 doses. URL: https://cellxgene.cziscience.com/collections/00109df5-7810-4542-8db5-2288c46e0424
- Open Problems NeurIPS 2023 perturbation prediction: PBMCs, 144 compounds, 3 donors, immune cell types. URL: https://openproblems.bio/results/perturbation_prediction
- scPerturb: harmonized h5ad perturbation datasets, 44 datasets in the paper/resource. URL: https://projects.sanderlab.org/scperturb/
- PerturBase: 122 scPerturbation datasets from 46 studies, ~5M cells, 24,254 genetic and 230 chemical perturbations. URL: https://www.perturbase.cn/
- PerturbSeq.db: 165 scRNA-seq and 24 scATAC-seq perturbation datasets. URL: https://bioailab.com/PerturbSeq.db/
- Disease atlases useful for CD4 T-cell drug discovery include UC SCP259/GSE? HCA, Crohn GSE134809, RA AMP RA/SLE/immunogenomics, SLE GSE174188/CELLxGENE, psoriasis GSE162183/GSE146264/HCA, MS GSE133028/GSE138266/GSE216749-related, and pan-cancer T cell/TME atlases.
